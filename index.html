Who am I?

At 26 years old, I have spent the majority of my career refining my sales skills in the industrial machinery world. Having worked on projects with international players such as Dart Container, Proctor and Gamble, and TREX I have refined my soft skills and cooperation acumen to facilitate a change of careers into the tech world. Although most would not consider sales as any way analogous to computer programming, the basic interaction skills required to work in a team of developers translates nearly 1:1. The face-to-face assertiveness required to tell a customer their project is going to cost them half a million dollars more than they thought or that their project has been delayed by 3 months translates well to stepping into a leadership type role without being a steamroller.
